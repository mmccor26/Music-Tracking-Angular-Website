DMCA Workflow and Tools

To Log & Update DMCA Dispute

  1. Log into your sitemananger account
  2. Fill in the required fields for the dispute
  3. Press the log dispute button and the dispute will be logged
  4. To update fill in the field along with the copyright ID associated with the dispute and press the update dispute button

To Enable/Disable DMCA Disputed Songs

  1. Enter the ID of the song you wish to enable or disable
  2. Click the Enable/Disable Button
  3. This will enable a disabled song and disable an enabled song
